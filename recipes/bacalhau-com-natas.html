Codfish with cream

Description

It is said Portuguese people can cook codfish in a 1001 ways. This is definitely one of my favourites! It is an oven-baked dish consisting of layers of codfish, onion, diced fried potato and cream. It is one of the most famous styles!

Ingredients

  • 500g codfish (Bacalhau)
  • 6 potatoes
  • 2 onions
  • 3 garlic teeth
  • 200ml cream
  • 200ml milk
  • Olive oil
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Peel the skin off of the chouriço and cut the chouriço into pieces. Place the chouriço, the peeled garlic and the oregano in a food processor and pulse until well chopped.
  2. Place the chouriço mixture in a bowl, add the grated cheese and the mayonnaise and mix well with a spoon.
  3. Cut off the top of the bread with a knife and remove the inside (reserve the bread crumb).
  4. Place the filling inside the bread and cover with the lid. Place the bread and the bread crumb on a baking pan
  5. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes. Remove the bread from the oven and serve hot.
  • If the cod is still not cooked, bake it until it is ready. Remove the bones and peel the skin.
  • Cut the potatoes in small cubes and fry them. Do not worry if they are not fully fried, as they will go to the oven for the final touches!
  • Cut the onions in thin slices, crush the garlic teeth and involve them with a bit of olive oil. Once the onion looks caramelized, add the codfish. Add salt and pepper "al gusto".
  • Put the milk, the cream and a tablespoon of butter in a pan and heat it, until the mix thickens a bit. Remove it from the stove.
  • Put the fried potatoes in a tray, together with the codfish. Mix them and involve well with the cream from step 4. Then put it on a tray or baking dish to bring to a preheated oven at 200ªC. Leave it for few minutes, until the top gets some color.
