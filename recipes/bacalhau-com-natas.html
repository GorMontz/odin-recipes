Codfish with cream

Description

It is said Portuguese people can cook codfish in a 1001 ways. This is definitely one of my favourites! It is an oven-baked dish consisting of layers of codfish, onion, diced fried potato and cream. It is one of the most famous styles!

Ingredients

  • 500g codfish (Bacalhau)
  • 6 potatoes
  • 2 onions
  • 3 garlic teeth
  • 200ml cream
  • 200ml milk
  • Olive oil
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. If the cod is still not cooked, bake it until it is ready. Remove the bones and peel the skin.
  2. Cut the potatoes in small cubes and fry them. Do not worry if they are not fully fried, as they will go to the oven for the final touches!
  3. Cut the onions in thin slices, crush the garlic teeth and involve them with a bit of olive oil. Once the onion looks caramelized, add the codfish. Add salt and pepper "al gusto".
  4. Put the milk, the cream and a tablespoon of butter in a pan and heat it, until the mix thickens a bit. Remove it from the stove.
  5. Put the fried potatoes in a tray, together with the codfish. Mix them and involve well with the cream from step 4. Then put it on a tray or baking dish to bring to a preheated oven at 200ÂªC. Leave it for few minutes, until the top gets some color.
